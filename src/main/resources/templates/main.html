Welcome to unofficial WebRHULCalendar.

To see your timetable you need to provide iCalendar link

How to find iCalendar link?
1 - Sign in in https://webtimetables.royalholloway.ac.uk
2 - Go to My Timetables
3 - Select Calendar Download
4 - Press View Timetable
5 - Press Android(tm) and others
6 - Copy bold link which starts with https://webtimetables.royalholloway.ac.uk/ical/........
7 - Add link to input field and press Submit
